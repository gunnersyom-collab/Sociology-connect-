Research Guide for Sociology & Social Work

Step-by-step procedure for writing a high-quality research paper:

  1. Choose a Relevant Topic: Select a sociological or social issue that interests you and is researchable.
  2. Conduct a Literature Review: Gather existing academic sources, articles, and books to understand the background.
  3. Formulate a Research Question: Develop a clear, specific question or hypothesis that your paper aims to address.
  4. Design Your Methodology: Determine your data collection method (e.g., qualitative interviews, observations, or quantitative surveys).
  5. Data Collection & Analysis: Gather your information from the field and analyze it to find key themes or patterns.
  6. Structure the Paper: Organize your work into standard academic sections: Introduction, Literature Review, Methodology, Findings, and Conclusion.
  7. Review & Cite Sources: Proofread your paper for clarity and ensure all references follow academic standards (e.g., APA style).
